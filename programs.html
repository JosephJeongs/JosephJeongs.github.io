Agario

Program Description

Agario is a fun short term game that is playable from anywhere. The user, once spawned in, will take the form of a tiny cell. The objective is to consume small pellets and other users to climb the leaderboard and become the biggest cell. Navigate your cell through means of directing it with the cursor of the mouse.

Rhythm Game

Program Description

Rhythm games are a popular form of a high concentration game. There are many styles, but this is the most common variation: 4 keys with notes falling from the top. Your goal is to complete the map without losing all your health.

Bird Catcher

Program Description

Baby bird catcher involves a player, the basket, and the goal of collecting 100 birds that rain from above. Navigate your basket horizontally to save as many as you can, but be wary! Spikes will accompany the birds that fall.
