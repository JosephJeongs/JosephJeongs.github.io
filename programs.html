1. Baby Bird Catcher

This program is a very simple game. As the user, you are controlling the basket that is near the bottom to the left or right. Simultaneously, birds and spikes fill continuously fall from above. The goal: collect 100 birds before you lose to coming into contact with a spike. There are also 4 difficulties to go along with the game, each increasing the number of spikes that can exist on the screen.
